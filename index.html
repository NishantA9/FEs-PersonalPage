Lord Shiva

Shiva is known as The Destroyer within the Trimurti, the Hindu trinity which also includes Brahma and Vishnu. In the Shaivite tradition, Shiva is the Supreme Lord who creates, protects and transforms the universe.

Lord Ganesha

Ganesha is a Hindu god with the head of an elephant and the body of a human. He is known as the remover of obstacles and is often depicted with four arms, a pot belly, and one broken tusk. Ganesha is also known as Ganesh or Ganapati.
